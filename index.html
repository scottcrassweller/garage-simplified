Our Story

Built out of
necessity.

Garage Simplified started the way most good ideas do — out of pure frustration. We're a family based in the Northwest Suburbs of Chicago, and for years our garage was the room we dreaded. Bikes falling over, totes stacked to the ceiling, the kids' gear mixed in with everything else. Every morning felt like a small disaster just trying to get out the door.

We tried the store-bought shelves. We tried the plastic bins. Nothing quite fit, and nothing quite held up. So we did what felt natural — we built our own. A custom wood storage system, designed around the way our family actually lives. The bins we actually own. The backpacks, the shoes, the sports gear. All of it, finally with a home.

The difference was immediate. Mornings got calmer. The garage stopped being a source of stress and started being a space we were genuinely proud of. Our neighbors noticed. Our friends started asking questions. And that's when we realized — this isn't just something we built for ourselves. It's something we could build for our community.

That's Garage Simplified. Real people, real families, real solutions — handcrafted right here in the suburbs we call home.
